Research Interests


Bioinformatics

With the ever-increasing development of synthetic biology and the pace of genomes currently being sequenced, there is a great need for fast and accurate computational tools that automatically identify 1) functional parts of DNA sequences, including promoters, terminators, transcriptional factor binding sites and ORFSs and so on; 2) Gene regulatory networks (especially, the hub genes) from microarray datasets; 3) diagnose diseases (especially, the cancer diseases) from microarray datasets. We are devoted to developing and using various data-mining and Statistics technologies for further improvement of such issues.

in silico Synthetic Biology

From 2000, Synthetic Biology is just forward developed by surprising speed. We are interested in using Chaos and other control theories to analysis the stability, responsibility and other Dynamic properties of Gene regulatory networks.

The original process control system

The main interest in such field is about using data-driven methods to improve the performance of quality control system. Of course, PLC, DCS and other original process control system are within our research interests.
